## Idea

https://urls2.blog is a simple blog writer for your urls, bookmarks and interests. The app is simple:

- You log in with your Google account
- You paste a url into the url field
- You click "Save" to add the url
- A modal appears with the options on blog, bog reason, tone, format, tags, category and additional instructions
- You click "Generate" to generate the blog post
- The blog post appears in the editor
- You can edit the blog post and click "Save" to save it
- In the url feed you can generate a blog post for any url you want, even if you already generated one for that url
